Mission

       Many diseases such as cancer and diabetes (visible health problems) are deeply tied to dysfunctions in intracellular system (invisible nanoscopic phenomena). The intracellular system is rigorously controlled by biochemical reactions. If some dysfunction occurs in the system, it occasionally leads to a serious situation that is considered as unstable intracellular system. A fundamental therapeutic method for the disease will be required to control the intracellular system that is controlled by the change in concentrations of biochemical molecules. To this end, we propose the following remedies.

(1) Control theory for regulating a concentration of a target molecule to a desired level by using      feedback control mechanism.
(2) Design method for creating a permeable membrane with a desired transmission rate on an      impermeable membrane.

With (1) and (2), we will be able to control intracellular system, and that technique makes unstable cancer cell into a normal cell. However, in order to drive a cell system, we first have to establish a technique to access the intracellular system. The remedy (2) is a possible solution, and would be based on a simple idea. An exiting application is shown in our movie. Please check it.

What is Molecular Governor?

       Almost all products, powertrains, and machines in our lives are built upon various control technologies. This fact is really amazing. However, more amazingly, the control technology has originated as a governor that was made by James Watt in 1769 during the industrial revolution. The technology was born so as to regulate a rotational velocity of centrifugal governor, and initially a simple mechanism. The concept or basis have been extended to a broad kind of machines for a long time, and finally it has become a control theory. Now, we try to make a simple basis to regulate a concentration of a target molecule. It would be just a small step to control biochemical reaction systems. But, we gave our proposal by the name of “Molecular Governor”, having a strong desire to future expansion. We believe that it will change our lives in the future as Marco would have achieved in our movie.

Theory

       This movie shows the state that the density of molecules is controlled through a membrane, and we will control the density of molecules x2 existing in there.Molecules x1 existing outside a membrane react with signal molecules e1. Then, molecules x2 is generated.Molecules x2 can permeable a membrane freely and go in and out of that.Also, the density of molecules x2 is changed by that of signal molecules e1 from the outside.

       As for the membrane, there are holes to penetrate molecules, and molecules x2 are used for a reaction with molecules G, and generate molecules H.Thus, molecules x2 penetrate a membrane in the ratio of uniformity and are used equally. Thereby we can make the density of molecules x2 whose are in the membrane a constant value.

Let xp be defined by

\begin{align} {x}_p ={x}_2+{x}_4\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c} \tag{2} \end{align}

By differentiating Eq.(3) and we have (4),

\begin{align} \dot{{x}_p} = \frac{{V}_2{x}_1{e}_1}{{K}_2+{x}_1}-\frac{{V}_5{x}_4}{{K}_5+{x}_4}\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c} \tag{3} \end{align} \begin{align} \dot{{x}_p} = {V}_2{e}_1-\biggl(\frac{{V}_5}{{K}_5}\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c}\biggl){x}_4 \tag{4} \end{align}

Now, we consider a steady state of xp. Then, we obtain

\begin{align} x^*_4 = \frac{{V}_2{e}_1}{\frac{{V}_5}{{K}_5}\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c}}=\frac{{V}_2{V}_c{K}_5}{{V}_5{V}_n}{e}_1 \tag{5} \end{align}

By calculating the following equation,

\begin{align} {x}_4-x^*_4 = {x}_4-\frac{{V}_2{e}_1}{\frac{{V}_5}{{K}_5}\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c}}= \frac{1}{\frac{{V}_5}{{K}_5}\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c}}\biggl(\frac{{V}_5}{{K}_5}\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c}{x}_4-{V}_2{e}_1\biggl) \tag{6} \end{align}

We have

\begin{align} \therefore\dot{{x}_p}= -\frac{{V}_5}{{K}_5}\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c}({x}_4-x^*_4)= -{K}_I({x}_4-x^*_4) \tag{7} \end{align} \begin{align} \biggl({K}_I=\frac{{V}_5}{{K}_5}\frac{{V}_n}{{V}_c}\biggl) \end{align}

From (7)and(8), we obtain the following system, where α and β are positive constants.

\begin{align} \therefore\dot{{x}_4}= -\alpha{x}_4(t)+\beta{x}_p(t) \\{x}_p={K}_I\int_{0}^{t}({x}_4(t)-x^*_4)dt \tag{9} \end{align}

Equation.(9) shows that x4 reaches to the target concentration x4* by the action of xp.
