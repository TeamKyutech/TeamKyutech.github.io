Membrane

Membrane as a key element in pur devise

A technical highlight of our product is to realize an integral controller for regulating a concentration of a specific DNA strand as an output signal to be a desired level by using a series of DNA strand displacement reactions in combination with a membrane. Therefore we attempted to design of osmosis membrane that DNA strand can move.

Fig.1 indicates that flow of a single DNA strand and multi-stranded DNA through a membrane. As shown in Fig.1, a specific single-stranded DNA can permeate membrane since the size of single-stranded DNA is enough to move the pore of membrane. However, the multi-stranded DNA can’t permeate membrane since it is much longer than single-stranded DNA. Because of the membrane, we can regulate a concentration of DNA strand.
