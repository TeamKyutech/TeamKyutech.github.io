Abstract

       A cell in our body is a certain type of molecular robot, and is controlled by an intracellular signal transduction system that is comprised of biochemical reactions. Some dysfunctions in the system occasionally result in a serious situation such as canceration. That is a control problem, and in a cancer cell, biochemical reaction system becomes unstable. Not just a cancer cell, various diseases are caused by some changes in the control system from the normal status. If we establish a technique to control a biochemical reaction system according to our purpose, it will open the door of breakthrough appreciation such as “smart” drug with no side effect for various diseases, and drastically change our way of life through a medical revolution.

       In BIOMOD2014, our team focuses on how to implant a control system in biochemical reactions. More precisely speaking, we propose (i) a design of reaction scheme that functions as a regulator to control a concentration of a target molecule, and (ii) an innovative method to create a function of a permeable membrane on a non-permeable one. See Design and Experiments pages for the details.
