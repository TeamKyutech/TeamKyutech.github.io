Material of Membrane

       We described that necessary of membrane and design theory before. In this section, we examine following things by experiment.


● Whether we can separate DNA through membrane with nanometer scale pore.
● Comparing concentration change between simulation and experiment.

       Recently, a number of authors have suggested about membrane technology. However, they have many problems to produce membrane. Especially, in this experiment, it’s difficult to handle such small membrane. Therefore, in order to solve them, we searched existing membrane and we apply it to simply experiment.

Fig.1  Conventional membrane


Problem


● It’s difficult to make a small hole such a    nanometer scale exactly in membrane.

● We have potential to tear a membrane by using    small membrane in experiment.

Fig.2 Ceramic membrane filter


Solution


● We can prepare membrane with nanometer pore by    using ceramic membrane

● Highly accurate separation of molecule

● Durability and Reusable many times

In our experiment, we used ceramic membrane filter of Nihon Gaishi company that provides membrane.

Method

       We examine the concentration change of DNA oligonucleotide after it permeates membrane by using above materials and equipment. (Fig4)

Fig.4  overall view of experiment 　gif

Procedure1


(i) Put water in water tank
(ii)Separate ceramic membrane with glass plate and put it in water tank

Procedure2


(iii)take a solution consists of DNA oligonucleotide and flow it in water tank.
(iv)Check the concentration change per unit time by using spectrophotometer.

Procedure3


(v)Run a simulation of concentration change with DNA and membrane which we use in experiment
(vi) Compare the concentration change between (iv) and (v).
